## Brand Metaphor

### Why "Puzzle" Works:
1. **Perfect Metaphor**: Every sprint is like solving a puzzle
2. **Visual**: Puzzle pieces = team members/resources
3. **Universal**: Everyone understands puzzles
4. **Memorable**: Simple, one-word name
5. **Professional**: Works in enterprise settings

### Brand Messaging:
- "Every sprint is a puzzle to solve"
- "Fit your resources perfectly"
- "Complete the picture"
- "Every piece matters"
- "Solve your capacity challenges"

## Future Branding Opportunities
